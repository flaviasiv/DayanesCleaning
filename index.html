Services

Dayane's Cleaning provides superior cleaning services for homes, apartments, townhouses, offices, business spaces and more throughout the metropolitan area. Our cleaners are enthusiastic about cleaning your home soon and showing you the difference that the Dayane's team can make.

Residential
Cleaning

Get this service done quickly and efficiently by the experts here at Dayane's Cleaning. We understand how important it is to feel confident and worry free when it comes to the service we provide. Our dedicated staff invests the time and energy necessary to be well prepared for any unique requests or special concerns our clients may have.

Move-in/Move-out/
Airbnb's

Pre or Post Occupancy cleaning for residential and commercial space. Moving cleanings are more thorough than regularly scheduled cleanings. Our team can clean areas that might not regularly get touched during regularly scheduled cleanings such as inside cabinets, inside the oven, refrigerator, wiping all baseboards, full dust from top to bottom, spot cleaning of walls. Window cleaning available by request. Fully customizable to meet your cleaning needs.
